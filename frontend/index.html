Incident Review Brief

A browser-based workflow was observed with unusually low hesitation and highly regular click cadence. Review the summary, consider the timing patterns, and submit a short interpretation of whether the interaction appears human, expert-driven, or automated.

VERITX expects natural sessions to include a reading phase, micro-pauses, occasional corrections, and non-uniform mouse movement. The system now backs off when the session is too thin, keeping weak evidence from turning into overconfident conclusions.

  • Look for timing irregularity, not just speed.
  • Consider if the sequence follows READ -> PAUSE -> TYPE -> CORRECT -> SUBMIT.
  • Use the dashboard to compare risk, confidence, evidence quality, and counterfactuals.
